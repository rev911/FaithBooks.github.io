The Messianic Secret
What Jesus Didn't Want Some People To Know

Why did Jesus tell His followers NOT to tell anyone who He was? This is a detailed study of the Messiah. It looks at Jewish history, the methods of interpreting Old Testament prophecy, and some contemporary views. It is designed to give the Christian a deeper understanding of the Messiah and His mission.
